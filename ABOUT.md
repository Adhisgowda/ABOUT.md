# About Heiofp
**Health Is Our First Priority.**

Heiofp is built to stand as the most transparent, honest, and high-velocity digital healthcare utility platform in India. Our name reflects our core foundational philosophy: **Health Is Our First Priority**.

### Built for India, Crafted in Chikmagalur
Heiofp is proudly engineered by a solo developer operating from rural Chikmagalur, Karnataka. It represents a grassroots "Made in India" initiative, proving that world-class, highly secure financial escrow architectures and deep digital health integrations can be crafted directly from rural India to solve nationwide healthcare challenges.

### Mission & Innovation
By pairing an automated, non-custodial Escrow Payment system with deep Ayushman Bharat Digital Mission (ABDM) infrastructure, Heiofp re-imagines patient-provider interactions. We guarantee that patients never lose money on treatments they do not receive, while simultaneously equipping medical facilities with real-time slot optimization, ABDM "Scan & Share" instant queue management, and automated no-show management tools.

### Transparency & Legal Right to Update
We are dedicated to transparency, scale, and administrative clarity. To stay aligned with fast-evolving healthcare regulations and digital frameworks, Heiofp explicitly maintains the architectural right to modify, change, adapt, or update its entire legal ecosystem, including all terms, conditions, privacy clauses, and operational rules, at any time.
