# About Heapiq
# Heapiq - Smart Live Health Queue Router 

###The Meaning Behind Our Name
Heapiq is a powerful combination of two core pillars: Health (Heap) and Intelligence (IQ). In computer science, a "Heap" is a smart, highly organized data structure used to sort and manage priority queues instantly. At Heapiq, we bring that exact algorithmic intelligence to the healthcare ecosystem—transforming chaotic waiting rooms into streamlined, real-time digital queues.

###Our Mission
Heapiq is India’s next-generation digital healthcare router. Integrated seamlessly with the government’s Ayushman Bharat Digital Mission (ABDM) framework, we provide a unified platform where patients can securely manage family health identities, instantly search verified local providers, and book digital appointments with absolute ease.

###What We Solve
We believe that no citizen should have to waste hours standing in overcrowded, unpredictable hospital lines. By utilizing advanced, crowd-sourced live data learning loops, Heapiq provides patients with real-time token tracking and precise arrival updates. Simultaneously, we empower independent private clinics with automated operational dashboards to manage daily queues on autopilot. At Heapiq, we are making Indian healthcare smarter, one token at a time.

### Built for India, Crafted in Chikmagalur
Heapiq is proudly engineered by a solo developer operating from rural Chikmagalur, Karnataka. It represents a grassroots "Made in India" initiative, proving that world-class, highly secure financial escrow architectures and digital health integrations can be crafted directly from rural India to solve nationwide healthcare challenges.

### Mission & Innovation
By pairing an automated, non-custodial Escrow Payment system with deep Ayushman Bharat Digital Mission (ABDM) infrastructure, Heapiq re-imagines patient-provider interactions. We guarantee that patients never lose money on treatments they do not receive, while simultaneously equipping medical facilities with real-time slot optimization, ABDM "Scan & Share" instant queue management, and automated no-show management tools.
